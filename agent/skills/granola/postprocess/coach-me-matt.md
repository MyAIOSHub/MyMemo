# Coach Me Matt — Mochary-Style Executive Coaching

## Mochary Method Curriculum (reference)

**ACT Foundation:** Accountability, Coaching, Transparency.
- **Accountability** = clear destination (Vision, OKRs, KPIs) + actions + verify completion.
- **Coaching** = current state — what's working, what's not, proposed solutions.
- **Transparency** = open feedback to manager, peers, reports.

**Core teaching: fear and anger give bad advice.** Amygdala bypasses prefrontal cortex. Shift out before acting (e.g., get curious instead of crushing someone).

**Effective Meetings:**
- Meeting Owner role
- Desired Outcome (written)
- Async prep (status, issues, data shared in advance)
- Time-boxed agenda
- Every decision → action item → DRI with due date, tracked centrally
- End with written feedback request

**Group 1-1s** instead of traditional 1:1s — saves time, increases transparency, faster decisions.

**5 A's of Receiving Feedback:**
1. **Ask** — actively solicit ("don't tell me, just think it… now tell me")
2. **Acknowledge** — repeat back until "that's right"
3. **Appreciate** — say thank you, no excuses
4. **Accept** (or not) — be transparent about decision
5. **Act** — co-create action item with due date

**Relationship Method (4 keys):** Ask, Prove you heard, Prove you remember, Let them know what you appreciate.

**Recruiting:** Anti-Sell early, speed signals conviction, top-grading reference checks (managers/peers from past jobs), spouse engagement.

**Onboarding:** Shadowing 30–60 days, then reverse-shadowing.

**Energy Audit (zones):**
- Incompetence (others better)
- Competence (others equal)
- Excellence (great at, hate doing — DANGER ZONE)
- **Genius** (great + love it — aim for 75–80% time here)

**Calendar Cadence:** 1 day internal meetings, 1 day external, 3 days no meetings (maker time).

**Decision Methods:**
- M1 (Low) — decide & announce
- M2 (Medium) — straw man + invite feedback
- M3 (High) — team brainstorm → straw man → final

**RAPID:** Recommend, Agree, Perform, Input, Decide.

**Conscious Leadership:** Curious > right. 100% responsibility. Magic Questions in monthly 1:1s. Specific praise (action, not personality).
