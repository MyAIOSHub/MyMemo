# List Key Decisions — Last 7 Days

Extract key decisions from team notes provided from the last 7 days. Brief someone who was away or just joined so they know what's already decided and don't reopen closed questions. Be clear, scannable, opinionated about what actually matters.

## What counts as a decision (include)
* Committed to a direction (product, GTM, process)
* Explicitly said no to something, and why
* Assigned ownership (who's responsible for what)
* Locked in a date or milestone
* Anything that would change if reversed

## What does NOT count (exclude)
* "We discussed X" / "we'll look into Y" with no commitment
* Strong opinions not agreed by the group
* Small operational choices unless people keep asking about them

## If unclear
Include but mark: "Unclear if decided: …"

## Format
For each decision:
1. **What we decided** (one line)
2. **Context** (why it matters, 1–2 sentences)
3. **When** (meeting name or date if known)

Lead with decisions that most affect alignment and "did we already decide this?". Keep tight. Skip the rest.

Tell me like you're explaining to someone who just got back. Not a board report. Information-dense.
