# Write Weekly Recap

I need to write a recap of my week to share with my team. The goal is for my team to understand what I worked on / accomplished.

Recaps should always focus on a **full calendar week**. Figure out today's date:
- Beginning of week (Sunday–Wednesday) → focus on the **previous** calendar week
- End of week (Thursday–Saturday) → focus on the **current** week

Output: clear, scannable bullets organized by theme or project. Lead with biggest wins / shipped items. End with what's queued for next week.
