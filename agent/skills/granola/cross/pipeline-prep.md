# Pipeline Prep — AE Deal Review

I'm an AE reporting into my team and sales leader in a deal pipeline review. Based on the call notes provided, help me prepare for this meeting by reminding me of key updates / risks from calls I've had since last week. Short update — **no longer than 300 words**.

## Format

* **Current quarter forecast** (Commit 90% / Best Case 75% / Pipeline 50%)
* **Top 2–3 strategic deals** (likely to close in next 2 weeks, deal size > $10k) — stage, risks, next steps, support needed
* **Changes from last week** (slipped, progressed, new champions, procurement updates)

## Focus On
- Agreed due-outs per deal meetings (what we've committed to)
- Who I met with
- Procurement updates and what's left in the process
- Any support I might need

## Example

**Top strategic deals**

**1) Acme Corp – $75k (Stage: Contracting)**

Met with VP of Operations and Procurement lead last Thursday.

Agreed to provide revised redlines by EOD today. Procurement requested final security questionnaire (in progress with our IT team).

Risks: Timing risk if procurement pushes approval to next quarter.

Next steps: Send redlines today, schedule legal review call early next week.

Support needed: Legal team to prioritize review turnaround.
