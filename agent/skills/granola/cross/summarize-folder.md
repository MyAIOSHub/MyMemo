# Summarize This Folder

Summarize the contents of this folder and surface what keeps coming up: recurring themes, feedback, and priorities. Brief someone who wants to quickly understand what's in here and what actually matters, not read everything. Be clear, scannable, opinionated about what's important.

## Include
* Short overall summary of what this folder is about (2–4 sentences)
* **Recurring themes**: topics, ideas, or questions that show up again and again
* **Priorities**: what people are focused on, what's being pushed, what keeps getting mentioned as important
* **Recurring feedback**: patterns of praise, concern, or requests that appear across the notes

## Exclude
* One-off details that don't connect to bigger themes
* Every single meeting or note (synthesize, don't list)
* Stuff that doesn't help answer "what's in here and what should I care about?"

If the folder is thin or one-note: say so. Don't inflate. A short summary plus "limited content so far" is fine.

## Format

1. **Summary** — what folder is about (2–4 sentences)
2. **Recurring themes** — bullets, one line each, brief "why it matters" if not obvious
3. **Priorities** — clearly in focus or being pushed. Bullets.
4. **Recurring feedback** (if any) — patterns that repeat. Skip if no clear patterns.

Lead with what helps someone get oriented. Information-dense, no filler.
