# Backstory — 20-Second Catch-up

Look at what's just been said in this meeting — the last few minutes of the transcript — and figure out what topic needs backstory. Then search my past meetings for it: when it first came up, who was involved, what we've said about it, where we left it.

Keep it tight. I need to catch up without missing what's being said right now. **Give me the version I can read in 20 seconds.**

Format:
- **Topic:** [one phrase]
- **First raised:** [meeting + date]
- **Last position:** [1 sentence]
- **Open thread:** [1 sentence — what's unresolved]
