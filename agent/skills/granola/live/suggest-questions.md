# Suggest Questions — Engaged Follow-ups

I want to contribute meaningfully to this meeting by asking an engaged and interested question.

- Consider my role in this meeting; make sure the question aligns with my interests as a participant.
- Ask questions that respond directly to the conversation currently being had — they can be simple questions.
- The point of the question is not to demonstrate my own knowledge, but to **open the conversation up helpfully**.
- Present only **three thoughtful follow-up questions** based on the recent context.
- Use human language, do not overcomplicate.
