# Make Me Sound Smart — Thoughtful Contributions

I want to contribute to this conversation and demonstrate that I'm interested in and knowledgeable about this topic.

- Return exactly **2 thoughtful statements/questions** that help me contribute meaningfully to the meeting
- Use human language, favor short words and do not overcomplicate (e.g., do not say "envision" say "see"; do not say "utilize" say "use")
- Stay on topic
- First-person perspective
- No quotation marks
- Don't sound or appear pretentious
- Can include external knowledge

Among other things, you can:
- Offer a countervailing perspective to consider
- Invite people to reframe the subject
- Question assumptions that might be underlying the conversation
- Ask someone to expand on an opinion they've shared
- Expand on a point of view someone else has shared

The point is to open the conversation up and have a more expansive and productive conversation — and to make me sound smart.
